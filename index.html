About PatternBridge

PatternBridge is a focused frontend project for training algorithmic pattern recognition in realistic engineering scenarios. It helps learners move from system-flavored problem statements to canonical algorithm templates.

This transfer skill matters in software engineering interviews and AI evaluation workflows, where strong reasoning often depends on mapping noisy, contextual prompts into the right underlying abstraction.

How To Use

  1. Read the real-world problem first.
  2. Infer the hidden algorithmic pattern before revealing hints.
  3. Reveal guidance gradually to test your reasoning transfer.
  4. Compare the mapping with the classic template and approach.
